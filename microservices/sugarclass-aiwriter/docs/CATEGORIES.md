# Category Taxonomy

Complete category system with source recommendations for each age group.

---

## Overview

**12 Major Categories** covering diverse topics for age-appropriate content discovery.

**Design Principles**:
- Age-appropriate complexity (7-10 = simple, 17+ = advanced)
- Diverse topics beyond science/tech
- Free, full-article sources only (no paywalls)
- Visual content priority for younger ages

---

## Category Definitions

### **1. General News**
Current events, politics, world news, local news

**Age Suitability**: All ages (content varies by source)

**Sources by Age**:
- **7-10**: BBC Newsround, Scholastic News
- **11-13**: CNN 10, BBC News - UK, NPR News
- **14-16**: BBC News, CNN, Reuters, Guardian, NPR
- **17+**: BBC World, Reuters World, Guardian Politics, NPR Politics

**RSS Feeds**:
```
BBC News - UK: https://feeds.bbci.co.uk/news/uk/rss.xml
CNN Top Stories: http://rss.cnn.com/rss/edition.rss
Reuters World: https://www.reuters.com/rssfeed/worldNews
Guardian World: https://www.theguardian.com/world/rss
NPR News: https://feeds.npr.org/1001/rss.xml
```

## Age Group Recommendations

### **7-10 Years Old (Elementary)**
**Focus**: Visual learning, simple language, interactive content

**Recommended Categories**:
1. General News (simplified: BBC Newsround)
2. Science & Technology (NASA Kids, Nat Geo Kids)
3. Nature & Environment (Nat Geo Animals)
4. Arts & Culture (Smithsonian Kids)
5. History & Geography (History for Kids)

**Content Guidelines**:
- Short articles (< 500 words)
- Lots of images/videos
- Simple vocabulary
- Positive/uplifting tone
- No violence/politics

---

### **11-13 Years Old (Middle School)**
**Focus**: Expanding worldview, critical thinking introduction

**Recommended Categories**:
1. General News (CNN 10, BBC News)
2. Science & Technology (NASA, CNN Science)
3. Health & Medicine (KidsHealth)
4. Entertainment (IGN Kids, Polygon)
5. Sports (ESPN Kids)
6. Education (Edutopia)
7. Nature & Environment (Nat Geo)
8. Social Issues (PBS NewsHour)

**Content Guidelines**:
- Medium articles (500-1000 words)
- Mix of images and text
- Age-appropriate vocabulary
- Introduce current events
- Some complex topics OK

---

### **14-16 Years Old (High School)**
**Focus**: Academic depth, career exploration, global awareness

**Recommended Categories**:
1. General News (BBC, CNN, Reuters)
2. Science & Technology (Nature News, Ars Technica)
3. Health & Medicine (Mayo Clinic, WebMD)
4. Arts & Culture (Artnet, Hyperallergic)
5. Entertainment (Polygon, IGN, Variety)
6. Sports (ESPN, Bleacher Report)
7. Business & Economics (Forbes, TechCrunch)
8. Education (Inside Higher Ed)
9. Social Issues (ProPublica, Vox)
10. History & Geography (Smithsonian)
11. Nature & Environment (Nat Geo, Sci Am)

**Content Guidelines**:
- Long articles (1000+ words)
- In-depth analysis
- Academic vocabulary
- Global perspectives
- All topics appropriate

---

### **17+ Years Old (College/Adult)**
**Focus**: Professional depth, specialized interests, advanced analysis

**Recommended Categories**: All 12 categories

**Content Guidelines**:
- Research papers acceptable
- Technical terminology
- Complex analysis
- Diverse viewpoints
- No content restrictions

## Implementation in Application

### **Category Filter Sidebar**

```javascript
// static/app.js
const CATEGORIES = [
  'General News',
  'Science & Technology',
  'Health & Medicine',
  'Arts & Culture',
  'Entertainment',
  'Sports',
  'Business & Economics',
  'Education',
  'Lifestyle',
  'Social Issues',
  'History & Geography',
  'Nature & Environment'
];

function populateCategorySidebar() {
  const container = document.getElementById('categoryFilters');
  CATEGORIES.forEach(cat => {
    const btn = document.createElement('button');
    btn.className = 'category-btn';
    btn.textContent = cat;
    btn.onclick = () => filterByCategory(cat);
    container.appendChild(btn);
  });
}
```

### **Database Schema**

```sql
-- Categories stored as JSON array
CREATE TABLE articles (
    ...
    categories TEXT,  -- Example: ["Science & Technology", "Nature & Environment"]
    ...
);

-- Query by category
SELECT * FROM articles 
WHERE categories LIKE '%Science & Technology%'
AND age_group = '11-13'
ORDER BY published_date DESC;
```
